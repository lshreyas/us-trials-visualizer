The Story

America runs four in five trials worldwide.

In the mid-90s the US was the unrivalled center of clinical research. 400 of every 500 trial sites were American — Europe held most of the rest. China and India were rounding errors.

The Migration, In One Image

300 American sites changed hands
in three decades.

No single year decided it. Each year shaved a few points off the US share — to China, then India, then everywhere with patients and lower costs. The cumulative effect: a reshaped industry.
